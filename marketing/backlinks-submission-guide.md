Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Thanh, a software engineer based in France, and I built Noctalia because I've always been fascinated by dreams — but frustrated by how quickly they slip away.

🔐 Privacy-first — your dreams are encrypted, only you can see them

Currently on Android with iOS on the way. Available in 5 languages (EN, FR, ES, DE, IT).
