# Noctalia multilingual SEO goals - web research consolidation - 2026-06-04

Source GSC: `marketing/seo/search-console/2026-03-05_to_2026-06-02/`

Agents launched: EN, FR, DE, ES, IT web research agents.

Skills used in consolidation:

- `seo-audit`: protect against international SEO mistakes, cannibalization, weak locale pages, medical/predictive claims, and wrong-language intent.
- `content-strategy`: prioritize searchable intent, hub/spoke clusters, product-led pages, and internal linking.
- `copywriting`: turn SERP-native language into titles, meta descriptions, H1/H2, and CTA copy that can earn clicks.

## Global read

The international issue is not "English or French translation quality" alone. Each language has a different search market:

- ES already has volume and page-one-ish positions. The best one-week lever is CTR.
- IT has decent visibility but weak titles on concrete dream scenarios. The best one-week lever is scenario-led CTR.
- FR has better CTR but too little top-10 depth. The best one-week lever is content depth and internal links.
- EN should stop chasing generic dream meanings and build product-intent pages.
- DE has one proven hub query: `traumsymbole a bis z`. The best one-week lever is to turn that into a German lexicon cluster without URL cannibalization.

One-week goal: move the combined CTR toward 0.60% after recrawl. At stable impressions this would be about 1,080 clicks, or +448 incremental clicks over the period.

- GSC shows strong visibility: positions around 7-8 on the selected pages.
- SERP research shows Spanish pages win clicks with `Que significa sonar con...` plus concrete scenarios.

## Goal IT - scenario-led CTR on `fuoco` and `cane`

Baseline selected pages: 96 clicks, 27,174 impressions, 0.35% CTR.

One-week goal: move these two pages toward 0.60% CTR after recrawl. At stable impressions this would be about 163 clicks, or +67 incremental clicks over the period. Language-wide, moving IT from 0.40% to 0.60% would be about +190 clicks over the period, but that is broader than this one-week sprint.

Why this goal:

- GSC shows both pages are near the useful threshold: `/it/simboli/fuoco` pos. 10.4 and `/it/simboli/cane` pos. 9.6.
- Italian SERPs reward concrete variants: `cane che morde`, `cane che attacca`, `incendio in casa`, `fumo nero`.
- `smorfia` is a CTR clue in Italy, but should stay secondary to Noctalia's personal reflection positioning.

Risks:

- Do not let `smorfia` dominate the page; it can be a short section, not the brand promise.
- Avoid title stuffing such as `significato interpretazione numeri smorfia sogni`.
- Use natural Italian phrasing: `cosa significa`, `che vuol dire`, `se morde`, `in casa`, `fumo nero`.

## Goal DE - turn `Traumsymbole A-Z` into a lexicon cluster

Baseline selected pages: 103 clicks, 8,583 impressions, 1.20% CTR.

One-week goal: keep the winning hub URL, reposition it as `Traumlexikon A-Z`, and strengthen existing symbol pages instead of creating competing guide URLs.

Why this goal:

- The query `traumsymbole a bis z` already works: 57 clicks, 896 impressions, 6.36% CTR, pos. 4.1.
- Broader terms are weak: `traumlexikon` pos. 18.2, `traumdeutung lexikon` pos. 50.9.
- Existing DE pages already exist for `fallen`, `hund`, `tuer`, `verfolgung`, and `zaehne`; creating new `/de/guides/traumdeutung-*` URLs would likely cannibalize them.

H2/FAQ checklist:

- Hub: `Traumsymbole nach A-Z`, `Haeufige Traumsymbole`, `Traumdeutung nach Kategorien`, `So deutest du Symbole im Kontext`, `FAQ zum Traumlexikon`.

- Avoid medical or prophetic certainty. Use German qualifiers like `kann bedeuten`, `im Kontext`, `kein Ersatz fuer Beratung`.
- Decide tone consistency: DE should likely use `du` because Noctalia is app-native.

## One-week execution order

If only one consolidated week is available, execute in this order:

1. ES CTR titles/metas/H1/H2 on the 5 selected pages. Fastest expected click upside.
2. DE hub + existing symbol page enrichment. Best strategic cluster signal.
3. FR ranking-depth work on maison/dictionary/journal/recall + link to existing AI page.
4. IT finish `fuoco` and `cane`, then only add two satellites if the core pages are complete.
5. EN product pages: optimize `/en/voice-dream-journal` and `/en/ai-dream-interpretation-app`; create `/en/private-dream-journal-app` only if it can be substantial.

Measurement:

- Annotate the publication date in the sprint doc.
- Request GSC recrawl for changed URLs.
- Compare page + query data after 7, 14, and 28 days.
- Do not judge by page-level position alone; measure each page's target query set.
