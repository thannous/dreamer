# Google Play ASO Copy Pack - Noctalia - 2026-05-20

## Goal

Improve qualified Android conversion from organic search, web comparison pages, and Google Play visitors. Position Noctalia as an Android-first dream journal for fast voice capture, AI reflection, generated dream images, recurring symbols, Dream Chat, and privacy-conscious self-reflection.

## Positioning

Noctalia is for people who wake up with a dream already fading. The app should sound practical and trustworthy: record the dream, keep the details, explore patterns, and remember that dream interpretation is a reflection tool, not a medical diagnosis or prediction.

## A/B Test Hypotheses

1. Voice-first screenshot first should convert better than interpretation-first because it matches the strongest pain: dreams fade quickly.
2. A privacy line in the first 800 characters should improve qualified installs from comparison pages.
3. "Reflection tool, not diagnosis" should reduce mistrust around AI dream interpretation without weakening the value proposition.
4. Localized keyword order should vary by country: dream journal terms first in English/French, dream interpretation terms first where search intent is broader.
