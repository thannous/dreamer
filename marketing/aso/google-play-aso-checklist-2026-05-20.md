# Google Play ASO Checklist - Noctalia - 2026-05-20

## Goal

Improve qualified Android conversion from organic search, web comparison pages, and Google Play visitors. Noctalia should be positioned as an Android-first dream journal for fast voice capture, AI interpretation, generated dream images, recurring symbols, Dream Chat, and privacy-conscious reflection.

## Store Metadata Drafts

### Short Description Candidates

1. Voice dream journal with AI interpretation, symbols, images, and Dream Chat.
2. Record dreams by voice, explore symbols, create images, and find patterns.
3. Private AI dream journal for voice capture, dream art, and reflection.

### Long Description Structure

1. Open with the half-awake capture problem: dreams fade within minutes.
2. Explain the core workflow: record by voice or text, get transcription, receive structured AI reflection, review symbols, generate an image, continue in Dream Chat.
3. State trust boundaries clearly: reflection tool, not medical diagnosis or prediction.
4. Explain privacy in plain language: no sale of personal data, no targeted ads, audio is used for transcription flow, account/dream data can be deleted.
5. Close with Android-first CTA and premium value: recurring symbols, images, chat, statistics, reminders, and long-term journal.

## Screenshot Priority

1. Voice capture: show the record screen and the promise "Capture it before it fades".
2. AI interpretation: show structured analysis with themes, emotions, and reflection questions.
3. Dream image: show a generated visual card from a dream.
4. Symbol dictionary: show a symbol page plus recurring-symbol context.
5. Dream Chat and statistics: show follow-up conversation or pattern tracking.

## Localized Keyword Angles

- English: dream journal, dream interpretation, AI dream app, dream dictionary, lucid dreams.
- French: journal de reves, interpretation reve, dictionnaire des reves, reve lucide, IA reve.
- Spanish: diario de suenos, interpretacion de suenos, diccionario de suenos, sueno lucido, IA suenos.
- German: Traumtagebuch, Traumdeutung, Traumsymbole, luzides Traeumen, KI Traumdeutung.
- Italian: diario dei sogni, interpretazione sogni, dizionario sogni, sogno lucido, IA sogni.

## Trust And Proof

- Add a store screenshot or description line for "reflection tool, not diagnosis".
- Mention "available on Android" consistently; do not imply iOS availability until shipped.
- Use social proof only when verified from current store data.
- Ask for reviews after a meaningful moment: after the user saves several dreams or receives a useful analysis, not immediately after install.
- Target 20-50 qualified Android reviews from real users who have tested voice capture, image generation, and Dream Chat.

## Weekly ASO Loop

1. Export Google Play acquisition and conversion data.
2. Compare web page clicks to Google Play listing visits and installs.
3. Review top search terms and install conversion by country/language.
4. Update one metadata element at a time: short description, first paragraph, screenshot order, or localized copy.
5. Record date, change, baseline, and result after 7, 14, and 30 days.
