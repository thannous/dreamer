<h1 class="font-serif text-3xl md:text-5xl mb-6 text-transparent bg-clip-text bg-gradient-to-b from-white via-dream-lavender to-purple-400/50 leading-tight">Exam dreams: meaning when you fail, pass or feel unprepared</h1>
<p class="text-lg text-purple-200/80 leading-relaxed">You sit down, turn the page, and realize you did not study. Or you pass an exam with surprising ease. Exam dreams are common because they turn evaluation anxiety into a simple scene: someone is testing you, and you are not sure you are ready.</p>
</header>

<section aria-labelledby="quick-answer-title" class="glass-panel rounded-2xl p-6 mb-8 border border-dream-salmon/20 bg-white/5">
<h2 class="font-serif text-xl text-dream-cream mb-3" id="quick-answer-title">Quick answer</h2>
<p class="text-purple-100/80 leading-relaxed">Dreaming about an exam usually reflects fear of evaluation, pressure to perform, perfectionism, or feeling unprepared. During finals season or before a work review, the dream often mirrors real stress. Passing the exam suggests confidence or relief; failing often points to fear of judgment rather than actual failure.</p>
</section>

<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-3">Why exam dreams happen</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">Exam dreams appear when your mind is processing performance pressure. The test may represent school, work, family expectations, a relationship decision, or your own inner critic. What matters is not the exam itself, but the feeling of being measured.</p>
</div>

<div class="dream-card glass-panel rounded-xl p-6 border border-transparent">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-3">Dreaming you did not study</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">This is the classic unprepared dream. It often appears before deadlines, finals, interviews, presentations, or any situation where you fear being exposed as not ready enough.</p>
</div>

<div class="dream-card glass-panel rounded-xl p-6 border border-transparent">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-3">Dreaming you pass an exam</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">Passing an exam in a dream can point to relief, growing confidence, or a sense that you have already done enough preparation. It may also show that a challenge feels less threatening than it did before.</p>
</div>

<div class="dream-card glass-panel rounded-xl p-6 border border-transparent">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-3">Dreaming you fail an exam</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">Failing in the dream usually reflects fear of judgment. It does not mean you will fail in real life; it means your mind is rehearsing the feeling of not meeting a standard.</p>
</div>

<div class="dream-card glass-panel rounded-xl p-6 border border-transparent">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-3">Dreaming about finals</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">At the beginning of May, exam and finals dreams can intensify because calendars, revision, pressure, and comparison become more visible. The dream is often a stress signal, not a prophecy.</p>
</div>
</div>
<section class="glass-panel rounded-2xl p-6 my-10 border border-white/10">
<h2 class="font-serif text-2xl text-dream-cream mb-4">FAQ</h2>
<div class="grid gap-4">
<div class="glass-panel rounded-xl p-5 border border-white/10">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-2">What does it mean to dream about an exam?</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">It usually means you feel evaluated, judged, or under pressure to perform. The exam can symbolize school, work, family expectations, or personal standards.</p>
</div>

<div class="glass-panel rounded-xl p-5 border border-white/10">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-2">What does it mean to dream you did not study for an exam?</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">It reflects feeling unprepared, exposed, or worried that you have missed something important before a deadline or challenge.</p>
</div>

<div class="glass-panel rounded-xl p-5 border border-white/10">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-2">What does it mean to dream you pass an exam?</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">It can suggest confidence, relief, readiness, or the sense that you have handled a difficult test better than expected.</p>
</div>

<div class="glass-panel rounded-xl p-5 border border-white/10">
<h3 class="font-serif text-lg text-dream-cream mb-2">What does it mean to dream you fail an exam?</h3>
<p class="text-sm text-gray-300 leading-relaxed">It usually points to fear of judgment or perfectionism, not a literal prediction that you will fail.</p>
</div>
</div>
