Dream Journal: recall, methods & routines

Dream journaling helps you remember, structure, and reflect on your nights. This hub gathers the essentials: recall techniques, REM sleep basics, the meaning of common dream symbols, and habits that make the practice effortless.

Quick start

If you are new to this topic, focus on one small habit for a week. Consistency matters more than doing everything at once.

Start with recall, then add one practice. Keep notes simple: what you saw, how you felt, and what changed during the dream.

  • Review your notes and adjust.
