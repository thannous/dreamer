Oniri alternative for dream journaling

Updated May 20, 2026

Oniri is a mature dream journal and lucid dreaming product. Noctalia is the alternative to consider when you do not need a full lucid training suite and mainly want fast Android capture, AI reflection, generated images, symbols, and guided reflection.

Short version

Oniri should get credit for lucid dreaming depth: reality checks, WILD/MILD/SSILD, audio cues, statistics, PDF export, oral notes, and a strong iOS reputation. Noctalia should not claim to beat that toolkit. It competes as a focused Android dream-memory product for capture, interpretation, visuals and follow-up conversation.

Noctalia is a reflection tool. It does not provide medical, psychological, legal, financial or predictive advice.

Pricing evidence

Pricing note: Oniri App Store publicly lists Oniri Premium and full-version in-app purchases in the US listing and warns that pricing may vary by location. Noctalia owned pricing copy shows Free plus Onironaut at EUR 2.99/month or EUR 19/year. Present these as sourced signals, not as a universal cheapest-product claim.

Social proof and caveat

Public proof: on May 20, 2026, Oniri App Store showed 4.6 stars from about 3.6K ratings; Google Play showed 10K+ downloads and about 269 reviews. Its public copy also says the journal has been developed and tested for 10 years with dreamers. Noctalia should position honestly as newer, Android-first and more focused on capture-to-reflection.

Migration path

  1. Export PDFs or copy key dreams from Oniri before switching.
  2. Keep lucid alarms, techniques and reality-check routines in Oniri if they are central to your practice.
  3. Use Noctalia for new Android entries where speed, image memory and guided reflection matter more.
  4. Rebuild recurring symbols from recent dreams instead of trying to port every historical tag.

Choose Oniri if

Choose Oniri if lucid dreaming training, PDF export, advanced statistics and mature iOS support are your priority.

Choose Noctalia if

Choose Noctalia if you mostly want to remember dreams fast on Android, turn them into visual cards, follow recurring symbols, and talk through one dream at a time.

Do not switch if

Do not switch if lucid training is the habit you are protecting. Noctalia is a better replacement only when capture, reflection and visual memory matter more than Oniri techniques.
