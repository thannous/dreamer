Frequently asked questions

How does dream analysis work?

Noctalia uses advanced language models inspired by analytical psychology (Jung) and dream symbolism. The analysis engine spots recurring motifs and archetypes to give you lines of inquiry without pretending there is one single truth.

Are my dreams private?

Absolutely. Your recordings and notes are encrypted and stored securely. Only you can access them. We never sell your personal data.

Can I type my dreams instead of speaking?

Yes. If you prefer not to wake your partner or simply enjoy writing, a full text editor is available alongside voice capture.

Does the app work offline?

You can record dreams offline. Analysis and sync run automatically as soon as you reconnect.
